Android - SHA-1 fingerprint

Nicolas Anjoran
2020-08-27 (Updated: 2020-08-27)
android

Just a simple How-to that I found on StackOverflow. I tested it on Android Studio v4.

  • Open the Android studio project
  • Open the Gradle sidebar (on the right side of the screen)
  • Click on the project name
  • Click on Tasks
  • Click on Android
  • Double Click on signingReport
